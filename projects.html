Booking Information

A 25% deposit is required to secure a booking, with the remaining balance due before final delivery.

Deposits are non-refundable where a booking is cancelled without good reason. With at least 72 hours’ notice, a deposit can usually be transferred once to a new date.

Please ensure the property is ready to shoot at the agreed time. Where significant preparation is still required on arrival, additional waiting or preparation time may be charged.

If the property is not in suitable condition to photograph, or access and shooting conditions are not workable, the shoot may be shortened, rescheduled or cancelled at our discretion. Drone work is always subject to weather, permissions and safe operating conditions.

Floor plans are supplied for marketing purposes and should not be relied on as legal or survey documents. Travel outside the core working area may affect the final quote.

Project title

MW Photography

Professional photography for property, hospitality and commercial spaces.

We provide property, hospitality and commercial photography based on the South Coast, working across London, Hampshire and Dorset, with a focus on clean composition, natural light and accurate presentation.

Our work is created for listings, websites and marketing materials, with an emphasis on strong composition, clarity and imagery that feels polished without looking over-processed.

Services

  • Standard interior and exterior photography
  • Drone photography and aerial coverage
  • 360 tours and walkthrough-style content
  • Property videos and feature videos
  • Floor plans for listings and marketing packs

How we work

  1. Initial enquiry and project details
  2. Scope, quote and booking
  3. Shoot day and on-site capture
  4. Editing, finishing and digital delivery

Approach

We aim to produce bright, well-balanced imagery that feels true to the space while still presenting it at its strongest. Clear communication, reliable turnaround and consistent results are central to how we work.

Service area

Based on the South Coast, working across London, Hampshire and Dorset, with wider travel available on request.
